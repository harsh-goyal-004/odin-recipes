Lasagna



Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has gained widespread popularity worldwide due to its rich flavors and hearty composition. Traditionally, it consists of layers of wide, flat pasta alternated with fillings that typically include ground meat, cheese, and tomato sauce.

The meat sauce, often a rag√π made with beef, pork, or both, is simmered to develop a deep, savory flavor. Cheese, particularly ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan, is generously applied to add creaminess and richness. Each layer is carefully assembled, and the dish is baked until the top is golden and bubbling, resulting in a comforting, indulgent meal.



Ingredients :-

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 lb (450g) ground pork (optional)
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 oz each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
